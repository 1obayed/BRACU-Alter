Subteam Descriptions

🔌 Electronics Subteam

Works on the rover's electrical systems including sensor integration, circuit design and testing, battery pack management, power distribution planning, power budgeting, and PCB design to ensure reliable hardware operation across all rover subsystems.

⚙️ Mechanical Subteam

Responsible for designing and developing the rover's physical structure including chassis design, suspension and drivetrain planning, CAD modeling using engineering tools (SolidWorks, Fusion 360, Ansys), simulation and structural analysis, and testing.

🤖 AI & Control Subteam

Develops the rover's autonomous behavior and motion control systems including control system modeling, sensor-based decision making, path planning, feedback control (PID), ROS2-based robotic software integration, and embedded system interfacing.

📡 Communication & Vision Subteam

Works on computer vision and rover communication systems including camera feed processing, object and hazard detection, base station connectivity, router-to-router communication setup, telemetry transmission, and communication protocol implementation.

📊 Management & Outreach Subteam

Supports project coordination, documentation, scheduling, branding and promotional materials (Photoshop, Canva), sponsorship communication, outreach activities, innovation/startup competition participation, and maintaining external collaborations.
